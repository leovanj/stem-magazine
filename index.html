About The Centrifuge

The Centrifuge is a student-run STEM magazine that focuses on bringing clarity to complex scientific ideas. We help high school students frame their discoveries and share their unique perspectives.

Our Mission

To create a platform where high school students can share their research, insights, and passion for STEM with peers around the world.

What We Publish

  • Technical Tutorials - How-to guides and coding projects
  • Opinion Pieces - Perspectives on scientific developments
  • Interviews - Conversations with student researchers
